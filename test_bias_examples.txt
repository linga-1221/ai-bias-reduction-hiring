# Test Examples for Bias Detection

## Copy and paste these into the "Real-Time Job Description Bias Checker" to see how it works:

### Example 1: HIGHLY BIASED (Multiple Categories)
We are looking for a young, energetic rockstar developer who is strong, aggressive, and competitive. The ideal candidate should be a ninja programmer with dominant leadership skills.

Expected Detection:
- Gender Bias: strong, aggressive, competitive, dominant
- Age Bias: young, energetic
- Personality Bias: rockstar, ninja


### Example 2: GENDER BIASED (Male-oriented)
Seeking an assertive software engineer with competitive spirit and aggressive problem-solving abilities. Must be strong under pressure.

Expected Detection:
- Gender Bias: assertive, competitive, aggressive, strong


### Example 3: AGE BIASED (Youth-oriented)
Looking for a fresh, energetic developer who is a digital native with recent graduate enthusiasm.

Expected Detection:
- Age Bias: fresh, energetic, digital native, recent graduate


### Example 4: AGE BIASED (Senior-oriented)
We need a mature, seasoned, experienced professional with years of expertise.

Expected Detection:
- Age Bias: mature, seasoned, experienced


### Example 5: PERSONALITY BIASED
Join our fast-paced team! We need a guru who is a rockstar ninja at coding.

Expected Detection:
- Personality Bias: fast-paced, guru, rockstar, ninja


### Example 6: NO BIAS (Neutral Language)
We are seeking a skilled, qualified software developer with problem-solving abilities and technical expertise. The ideal candidate will be proficient in programming and dedicated to quality work.

Expected Detection:
- ✅ No Bias Detected


### Example 7: MIXED BIAS
Looking for a collaborative team player who is nurturing and supportive in mentoring junior developers.

Expected Detection:
- Gender Bias: collaborative, nurturing, supportive (female-biased)


### Example 8: SUBTLE BIAS
We want someone who is strong in algorithms, competitive in the market, and a rockstar at optimization.

Expected Detection:
- Gender Bias: strong, competitive
- Personality Bias: rockstar


## How to Use:
1. Open http://127.0.0.1:5003 in your browser
2. Scroll to "Real-Time Job Description Bias Checker"
3. Copy any example above
4. Paste into the text area
5. Watch the bias detection appear in real-time!

## Understanding the Results:

🟡 BIAS DETECTED = System found problematic words
✅ NO BIAS DETECTED = Language is neutral and inclusive

The system shows:
- Bias Score: Number of unique biased words found
- Biased Words: Highlighted in orange badges
- Category: Gender, Age, or Personality bias
